Pillow Design and Tutorial

For this project I was asked to locate an easy and accessible design program, learn it, come up with a product to create with it, and create a tutorial showing what I learned.

To create the tutorial, I broke the process step by step, then I used screen captures and edited them with prompts. Once this was compiled, I then developed the instructions.

Tools used:
Illustrator | Photoshop

Methodologies used:
