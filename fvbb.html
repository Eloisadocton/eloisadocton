STORY

The Fuquay Varina High School Band is small but mighty! What most people don't know is that the Town of Fuquay Varina has two school bands: the FV Middle School Band, and the FV High School Band. The directors of these bands are actually a wonderful married couple, who's dedication to the town's children, and it's musical development is inspiring. By the time the kids enter High School, they've been prepared in a very unique way to continue their music education in the High School, not to mention winning several music competitions.
Being a parent of two of it's members, and understanding the financial, logistical and daily challenges that this band experiences, plus witnessing the success of the program, where children even go to other states and towns and bring home first and second place trophies, I can't help but be involved, and super proud of these amazing kids!
I started by volunteering and adding event updates to the Booster's website, which is ran in Google Sites. After some time, and recognizing that the website needed, desperately, a makeover, I offered to update it. The tricky part of this was that the website needed to stay within Google Sites because this is a free platform for teachers and it worked well with the plugins that the band director needed.
I migrated the "Old" Google Site to the "New" platform, and, because I knew there were many parents who were used to the old site's flow, I decided to replicate some features that otherwise I would have dropped, for example, the hero image remained the same, and I added manually a left-navigation bar which the website in theory did not need it, but was going to help parents with a friendlier transition to the new platform.
I also purchased some beautiful and bold vector graphics to use for the events, which everyone also loved as they go very well with the school's theme.
