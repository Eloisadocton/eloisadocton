Eloisa Docton

For years, I have been a crafter. Which is what inspired me to start a career in Surface Design in 2008. My designs are used on fabrics and paper products throughout the world. I have licensed artwork with companies like Timeless Treasures, Casemate, Joann Fabrics, Cinnamon Joe Studio, Jelly the Pug, just to name a few.
My designs have also been featured in design shows such as Surtex and Blueprint {both generally in N.Y.}.
